about

Hi, I'm Ida!

I work across the fields of design, data and journalism.

I'm interested in the potential of graphics and data for investigating and making complex topics more tangible. I enjoy diving into different topics and I like exploring technologies and materials. I have worked with interactive installations and exhibitions, printed graphics and web design, data visualizations and data-driven journalism. Sometimes (not very often) I also write.
