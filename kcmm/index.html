Commissioned work

kids courage macht methoden

Design, layout and illustrations for a book

KidsCourage is an initiative working on democratic and anti-discrimination education, children's rights and empowerment. In 2019, they published a book about their pedagogy and a collection of their favourite exercises, which I created a layout design as well as illustrations for – based on a visual identity designed by İpek Erdöl.
