Data installations

Installations that translate data into space and context

Team: gewerkdesign

As part of my work at gewerkdesign, i worked on ideas and designs for translating existing data into three-dimensional installations for the Arolsen Archives. The Arolsen Archives are the central documentation hub about Nazi persecution and answer inquiries about victims of the same. The information we were asked to translate into installations mostly centered around these inquiries - the amount of inquiries per year, where the inquiries came from and the Arolsen Archive's international cooperation partners.
