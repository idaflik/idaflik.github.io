transitions festival

Visual identity

For the »TRANSITIONS. Festival of Contemporary Jewish Arts«, İpek Erdöl and I created a visual identity, print (posters, flyers, catalogue) and online (Website and Instragram) materials. To visually translate the festival's theme of transitions as "the liminal space, a “betwixt and between” state, where the usual order of things is disrupted and new potentials and limits can be challenged and explored", our designs used fragments that de- and reassemble into different shapes. Some elements, such as the gold and blue colors, were based on Dagesh, the host organization's branding.

The three-day festival presented a diversity of contemporary artistic perspectives and practices that navigate the space between protest and resistance, solidarity and reinvention. It invited artists and the public to celebrate the Art of Resistance and to a pan-European, trans-Atlantic exchange to explore new social conditions and visions.
