Visual identity for a furniture fair exhibition

Team: Simone Affabris, WenFu Chuang

In 2017, a group of students was chosen to exhibit work at the international furniture fairs in Stockholm and Milano. With two other students, I created a visual concept for our self-organized exhibition. We used dashed lines and negative space, which we applied both to the visual appareance of our exhibition space as well as the logo and various print materials.
