about

Hi, I'm Ida!

I work across the fields of data journalism, design and science.

I like to try and make complex topics more tangible and easy and engage with. I code and design, do research, file requests or scrape data, and make data visualizations, mostly. I work freelance and I'm part of the Remote Sensing Centre for Earth System Research at Uni Leipzig. Sometimes I teach or do workshops, too.

Don't hesitate to get in touch or find me on Mastodon!
