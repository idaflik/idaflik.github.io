Wo Wasser knapp wird - und wo nicht

Magazine

Team: Jonathan Dehn (Design), Knicker editorial team

The 21st edition of Knicker, a magazine format of German Katapult, focussed on water: water conflicts, water usage, water quality, freshwater supply and more. I calculated the number of days with exceptionally high or low amounts of water running through European rivers and extracted geodata for other visualisations. Find the whole issue here.
