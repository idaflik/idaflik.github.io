data and activism

Development, design and layout for playful exercises around digital security for activists

Team: Amber Macintyre, Leil Zahra

As part of my work at tactical tech, I collaborated on the „data and activism“ activity book. The project aims to create playful exercises that make people reflect on their data practices and involve with security aspects of the same. I co-created concept and content for a first prototype, and designed teaser cards as well as the published PDF chapters. The visual aesthetic for the project borrows from scrapbooking and activity book elements as well as the „data and activism“ identity colors and existing collage-style artwork.
